Wind Speed

There are a few items we can deduce from reviewing the latitude vs. wind speed graph above. First, the majority of wind speeds across the globe are less than 10 mph. Second, it appears that wind speeds in the northern hemisphere tend to be slightly higher than those in the southern hemisphere. Lastly, the lowest concentration of wind speeds appears to be at the equator.
